About Me

I'm Jordan — an IT engineer at Aerostar, where I design and build the infrastructure that keeps things running in the aerospace and defense space. My work focuses on identity management, cloud platforms, and making sure our systems meet the compliance standards that come with the territory — CMMC, classified environments, and the like.

Before Aerostar, I worked in biotherapeutics at SAb Biotherapeutics, managing infrastructure in ISO 7 cleanroom environments and navigating FDA compliance. And before that, I spent nearly seven years at Dakota State University — starting in tech support and working my way up to managing cloud systems and leading a team.

I studied Computer and Network Security at Dakota State, which gave me a solid foundation in the security-first mindset I bring to everything I build. I'm based in Sioux Falls, South Dakota.
